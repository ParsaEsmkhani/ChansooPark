Research & Collaboration

International partnerships across Canada, Korea, and beyond.

‹ ›

Dr. Chansoo Park is a Professor of International Business and Strategy at the Faculty of Business Administration, Memorial University of Newfoundland. His research explores international joint ventures, global strategy, and knowledge transfer across multinational organizations. Dr. Park brings global experience from South Korea, China, and Canada, and has contributed to leading journals such as Journal of World Business and International Marketing Review.
