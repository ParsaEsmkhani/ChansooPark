Research Overview

Dr. Chansoo Park’s research focuses on international business strategy, knowledge transfer, and corporate social responsibility. He has collaborated with scholars from multiple countries and examined multinational enterprises, joint ventures, and the role of local stakeholders in global business.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

Dr. Park has studied the effect of local stakeholder pressures on CSR strategies and implementation in multinational corporations. Key publications include:

  • Rhee, Y., Park, C., & Petersen, B. (2019). The Effect of Local Stakeholder Pressures on Responsive and Strategic CSR Activities. Business & Society.
  • Kasim, A., Yussuf, N., & Park, C. (2017). Application of corporate social responsibility in stakeholder management. International Journal of Research in Tourism and Hospitality.

Knowledge Transfer in International Joint Ventures

Dr. Park has extensively researched knowledge flows between parent companies and foreign subsidiaries, including tacit and explicit knowledge transfer:

  • Park, C., Vertinsky, I., & Becerra, M. (2015). Transfers of tacit vs. explicit knowledge and performance in IJVs. International Business Review, 24(1), 89-101.
  • Park, C., & Vertinsky, I. (2016). Reverse and conventional knowledge transfers in international joint ventures. Journal of Business Research, 69(8), 2821-2829.
  • Park, C., Vertinsky, I., & Lee, C. (2012). How the Exchange Climate Affects Tacit Knowledge Transfer from Foreign Parents. International Marketing Review, 29(2), 151-174.

International Tourism

Dr. Park’s research also examines the impact of institutional quality on tourism inflows and travel motivations:

  • Kim, Y., Shrabani, S., Vertinsky, I., & Park, C. (2018). The Impact of National Institutional Quality on International Tourism Inflows: A Cross-Country Evidence. Tourism Analysis, 23(4), 533-551.
  • Kasim, A., Dzakiria, H., Park, C., Mohd Nor, N.A., Mokhtar, M.F., & Rashid Radha, J.R. (2013). Predictors of travel motivations: the case of domestic tourists to island destinations in northwest Malaysia. Anatolia, 24(2), 188-205.

Strategic Alliances and Market Strategy

Dr. Park has contributed case studies and research on global expansion and strategic partnerships:

  • Park, C., Viswanathan, V., Parveen, S., Gopinath, R., & Furey, M. (2019). Costco Wholesale Corporation: Market Expansion and Global Strategy. Ivey Case 9B19M007.
  • Park, C., Oh, C., & Kasim, A. (2017). Market challenges, strategic orientations, and innovativeness in IJVs. International Marketing Review, 34(6), 945-967.

Executive Coaching

Dr. Park’s research also includes executive coaching and leadership effectiveness in global firms:

  • Park, C., & Cho, Y. (2014). Study on coaching: Key antecedents of executive coaching effectiveness for global firms. The Journal of Professional Management, 16(4), 25-43. (Korean)
